Culinary Delights: A Taste of Lucena

Lucena's food scene is a flavorful blend of tradition and local innovation. Prepare your taste buds for an unforgettable gastronomic journey!

Pancit Chami

The iconic thick noodle dish of Lucena, known for its savory sauce, pork, chicken, and fresh vegetables. A true local staple found in eateries all over the city.

Kamayan Sa Palaisdaan

Experience unique Filipino dining in a floating cottage. Savor fresh seafood, sinigang, and other traditional dishes amidst serene waters in nearby Tayabas City.

Lucena/Lucban Longganisa

A local sausage bursting with garlicky and savory flavors, a perfect breakfast staple often served with garlic rice and fried egg.

Budin (Cassava Cake)

Indulge in this delightful native delicacy of Quezon Province, known for its sweet and slightly chewy texture, often enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

Bubbles Crispy Pata

A renowned local spot famous for its perfectly crispy deep-fried pork hock, a favorite for family gatherings and celebrations.

Lucena Public Market Discoveries

Explore the bustling public markets for fresh produce, local ingredients, and an authentic glimpse into daily life and street food culture.
